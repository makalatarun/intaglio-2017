Operations Management

IIM Calcutta’s Intaglio in partnership with Uber proudly brings to you, the case based operations workshop aimed at understanding how the operations management has evolved over the past few years and how the companies in India have adapted to such change in the conventional process. The workshop will also focus on defining the importance of resources that needs to be addressed while dealing with operations. The workshop will also highlight the concept of Shared Economy which is well adopted by Uber and practiced by many companies in India. It indirectly involves keeping no inventory and sharing the resources.

Key Takeaways
  • A detailed understanding on the Uber's’ Operations
  • Knowledge sharing on key concepts of Operations & Shared economy by Instructors
  • Certificates will be awarded upon the completion of the workshop
  • Uber’s Coupons to the best presentations on the case to be discussed.
