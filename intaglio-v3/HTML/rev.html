Reverberation is a phenomenon of persistence of sound for some time after it has been stopped. This is precisely what we set out to achieve every year at one of the largest management summits of India. A scintillating series of talks by prominent personalities from diverse backgrounds. Global management thinkers and business leaders come down for Reverberations to provide perspectives and insightful ideas that resonate with the attendees for a long time.




Reverberations has a legacy of some amazing speeches by the finest leaders, thinkers, administrations, sportspersons, corporates, entrepreneurs etc. across the world.
