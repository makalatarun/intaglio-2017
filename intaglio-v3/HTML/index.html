ABOUT US

Intaglio


Intaglio, the annual international business summit of IIM Calcutta has been at the epitome of management excellence since 1989. Every year the stage is set for more than 15000 students across the world to showcase the practical ideas and solutions to managerial problems. Competitive events, enlightening talks by eminent personalities and sophisticated workshops by corporates all this with a lake-side view is assured. The ball is set rolling, come and witness the most awaited summit on 15-17th December 2017.

Tenets of Progressivism


Progressivism is a multi-facet ideology that advocates change and reforms with its roots in the 19th century Europe. It challenges the status-quo of the system and aggressively pushes the boundaries beyond the horizon. It describes both social and economic reforms that leads to the improvement in the human condition. For our current age, this philosophy can be categorized into 3 essential tenets namely – Inclusion, Education & Digitization.
