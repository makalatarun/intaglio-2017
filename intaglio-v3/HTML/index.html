PAST SPEAKERS

DR. SHASHI THAROOR

Dr. Shashi Tharoor is an Indian politician and a former diplomat who is currently serving as Member of Parliament of India. He also currently serves as Chairman of the Parliamentary Standing Committee on External Affairs. He is a member of the Indian National Congress

SHRIJEET MISHRA

Shrijeet Mishra, COO, Bennett Coleman & Company Limited, is a Wharton Alumnus and an economics graduate from Hindu College, Delhi, and he completed his management from IIM-Ahmedabad, for which he is extremely thankful for the foundation that was laid at DM and Ravenshaw.

SOURAV GANGULY

Sourav Ganguly, affectionately known as Dada, is a former cricketer and captain of the Indian national team. Currently, he is appointed as the honourable President of the CAB and President of the Editorial Board with Wisden India. He is regarded as one of India's most successful captains in modern times.
