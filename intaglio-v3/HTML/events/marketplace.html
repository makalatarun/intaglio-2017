"You can't just ask customers what they want and then try to give that to them. By the time you get it built, they'll want something new.” - Steve Jobs

Good marketing is more than the 7Ps and fancy campaigns, it can change the face of a whole industry and put the firm ahead in the Marketplace. This event tests your ability to make a full proof marketing plan that can give Kotler a run for his money. It is a battle to create a marketing strategy that will revolutionize the monotony of the Marketplace. Only the best laid plans can survive the marketplace.

  • ROUND 1

    A marketing online quiz that will test the team’s grasp on basic marketing concepts and current news, brand awareness, product communication strategies and marketing concepts.

  • ROUND 2

    Case study submission round revolving around a renowned brand which will test the creative minds of marketing enthusiasts. Teams have to come up with out-of- the-box ideas which are central to the brand and relevant to the target group or A simulation game. More details will be shared later

  • ROUND 3

    On-Campus case submission round for finalists will pitch their ideas to the panel during Intaglio 2017. Pitch will be followed by 3-5 minutes of Q&A
