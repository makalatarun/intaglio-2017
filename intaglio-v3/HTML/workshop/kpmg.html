Business Analytics Workshop

"The long range contribution of statistics is not to create a host of highly trained statistician. It is to develop a generation of statistically minded scientists, engineers, technicians and others who will run the … processes of tomorrow” – W.A.Shewhart and W. D. Deming

In today’s world while we are moving towards an era of concepts like Big data, the foundation of all decisions still remain to be plain and simple grasp of data and its applicability.. For all the aspiring professionals, the trick is to know, for a real world scenario, what tool to use and once the result comes , interpret the same for business environment and corporate decision making. Just like the journey of a mile begins with a single step, similarly, before going deep into the analytics it will be worthwhile to spend a couple of hours to go through/ refresh the basic tools that we may or may not have studied in the past and see how they are used for decision making by business leaders. The program will enable you to understand- What is data? Evolution of data Information vs data Basic Statistics 7 QC tools

Key Takeaways
  • Difference between data and information
  • Which statistical tool to used and when
  • How does the result translate into business decision
  • The basic 7QC tools
