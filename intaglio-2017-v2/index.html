ABOUT US

Intaglio is the Annual International Business School Summit of the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIMC). It is one of the largest business summit in Asia. Over the past twenty eight years, Intaglio has evolved to become a platform that brings together industry leaders, budding entrepreneurs, inspiring personalities, and motivated students to discuss, debate and re-think business and society. The summit occupies a much prided position in the calendars of the managerial intelligentsia worldwide and attracts participation from prominent industry leaders and students from over 200 schools and colleges with a footprint of over 16000 participants nationally and internationally.Intaglio is structured to comprise competitive events across various spheres of management such as Finance, Marketing, Policy, Strategy, Operations and Flagship domains with prizes worth more than INR 15 lakhs and speaker sessions on various themes. Over the years, Intaglio has been graced by esteemed speakers like Mr. Azim Premji, Mr. Montek Singh Ahluwalia, Mr. Sourav Ganguly, Dr. Bimal Jalan, Mr. Prahlad Kakkar, Mr. Shashi Tharoor, Mr. Prakash Jha, Mr. T.V. Narendran, Mr. Piyush Mishra et al. The speaker session is always star studded and all the participants really look forward to it. Our speaker series, Reverberations is designed to showcase eminent thoughts from different spheres of life - ranging from basic economics to sports management!
