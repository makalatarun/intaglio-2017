ABOUT US

Intaglio is the Annual International Business School Summit of the Indian Institute of Management Calcutta (IIMC). It is one of the largest business summit in Asia. Over the past twenty eight years, Intaglio has evolved to become a platform that brings together industry leaders, budding entrepreneurs, inspiring personalities, and motivated students to discuss, debate and re-think business and society. The summit occupies a much prided position in the calendars of the managerial intelligentsia worldwide and attracts participation from prominent industry leaders and students from over 200 schools and colleges with a footprint of over 16000 participants nationally and internationally.
Intaglio is structured to comprise competitive events across various spheres of management such as Finance, Marketing, Policy, Strategy, Operations and Flagship domains with prizes worth more than INR 15 lakhs and speaker sessions on various themes.
Over the years, Intaglio has been graced by esteemed speakers like Mr. Azim Premji, Mr. Montek Singh Ahluwalia, Mr. Sourav Ganguly, Dr. Bimal Jalan, Mr. Prahlad Kakkar, Mr. Shashi Tharoor, Mr. Prakash Jha, Mr. T.V. Narendran, Mr. Piyush Mishra et al. The speaker session is always star studded and all the participants really look forward to it. Our speaker series, Reverberations is designed to showcase eminent thoughts from different spheres of life - ranging from basic economics to sports management!

Intaglio-2017 : India Towards Epitome of excellence

From Nehruvian socialism to Modinomics, India seems to finally have woken up to life and freedom. From being a lumbering elephant to a running tiger, India is finally running at full pace and on its way to become the Epitome of Excellence for the world. Blessed with a favourable demographic dividend, business friendly policies, a push towards manufacturing - Make in India, a renewed focus on agricultural sector, India is set to become the fastest growing economy in the world. With other large economies slowing down or recovering from crises, India is poised to become the center of growth and excellence, leading the world by example.
There is a growing acknowledgement of India as a dominant power in the world today and countries around the world are recognizing the importance of India. The rising demands of including India as a permanent member of United Nations Security Council is a testament to that. India's successful completion of Mangalyaan mission to Mars' orbit on the very first attempt (first country in the world to do that) signifies our scientific abilities; eminent Indian origin people holding key management positions at Fortune 500 giants, home and abroad, reflects our business acumen; Yoga, Ayurveda, classical music, Nobel Peace laureats and multiple other cultural aspects paint India as an embodiment of Excellence in every aspect.
Contributing towards achieving excellence in all these fields are the various centers of excellence and reputed educational institutes in the country. We, at Indian Institute of Management Calcutta, recognize this fact and in its 29th edition, Intaglio 2017 celebrates the surge of India towards towering heights . We aspire to discover the best minds in different institutions, enable them and work with them to propel India towards reaching the Epitome of Excellence.
