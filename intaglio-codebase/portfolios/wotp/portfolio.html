About

Strategy formulation followed by its execution is what completes the analysis problem. Sports is that one area where you keep improvising your strategy along with executing those strategies. This event makes you test your managerial but sporting skills together. A fantasy football league with participating teams will join the league and compete against each other. So, put on your manager’s suit and take your team to the top! There are lots of prizes on offer

Event Format

Round 1

An online based multiple round fantasy football league on Hero Indian Super League (ISL), the newest Indian football league. Top scoring teams from all the registered teams would be invited for the final round on campus during Intaglio 2017

Round 2

An on-campus event that will be a simulation event where two teams would be competing against each other on a football management simulation. Before the final round begins, the teams would be given more information regarding the rules, format of the event and the software in a briefing session once they arrive on campus. The final round would start from a quarter final stage and then progress on to semis, final and the eventual winner

Event Rules

  • Registration is allowed in teams of size 3
  • Team members have to be from the same college
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • Individuals can not be a part of more than one team
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
