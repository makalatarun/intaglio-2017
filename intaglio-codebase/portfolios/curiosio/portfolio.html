About

Curioso is Intaglio’s online quiz, which is conducted online. The event aims to engage the best students across the world on Intaglio’s platform, and attracts participation from the world’s top universities

The event would be a multi-part event, conducted over several daysleading up to Intaglio; each round is independent. We have attractive prizes in cash and kind for winners of each round and the overall winners.

Event Format

Questions will be published in 5 slots with a deadline

Event Rules

  • This is an individual event
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • An individual can not register more than once
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
