About

The different facets of management have their own challenges and hurdles, but it takes a true leader to master all of them. The flagship event of Intaglio 2017, Olympus, will be the ultimate test for an individual through gruelling rounds of finance, strategy, marketing, analytics amongst other facets of management. Team Intaglio invites all the budding managers to be a part of this challenge to be at the top of Olympus.

Event Format

Round 1

An online quiz that tests participants on their aptitude in various fields of management as well as problem solving ability

Round 2

A video submission on a specific corporate situation / topic that may be complemented with a resume and SOP submission

Round 3

Multiple rounds involving finance, marketing, operations, HR and strategy which will be based on case studies, simulations as well as interactive setups

Event Rules

  • This is an individual event
  • This event is only open to students from business schools
  • Participants can register as individuals or in teams of 2
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • An individual can not register more than once
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
