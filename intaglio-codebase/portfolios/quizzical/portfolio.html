About

Quizzical is Intaglio’s flagship quiz, which is conducted on campus. The event aims to attract the best quizzing talent across India to showcase their prowess on campus, and has attractive cash prizes.

Event Format

Round 1

On campus preliminary round which will be evaluated on the spot

Round 2

On campus finale with several sub rounds which will be conducted by a quizmaster

Event Rules

  • Registration is on the spot, as individuals or in teams of sizes upto 3 members
  • This event is open to students in their graduation as well as post graduation
  • Team members have to be from the same college
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • Individuals can not be a part of more than one team
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
