About

The circulatory system of any company, the field of operations has always been subject to rapid technological innovation and dynamic processes. To synchronise the forecasts, supply, transportation, inventory levels and reverse transportation requires mammoth organisational and coordination skills that are not easy to master. Operationalise challenges participants on the grounds of theory, puts them through a simulated supply chain environment and a corporate case study to test their abilities as operations managers. Efficiency mixed with effectiveness is the key to performing well

Event Format

Round 1

An online quiz testing the theory and application of operations

Round 2

A simulation game based on supply chain management

Round 3

An in-campus case presentation
