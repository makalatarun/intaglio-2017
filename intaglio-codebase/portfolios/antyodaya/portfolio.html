About

Antyodaya, a Pan-Asian competition, is a unique event which aims to reward Indian and foreign students who have been working in the social sector in Asia. The vision of the event is to promote solutions for those at the Bottom of Pyramid. In its 3rd edition, the event provides the participants with a platform to showcase their endeavor to a large audience comprising Corporates – Capable of taking the efforts to the next level as a part of their CSR initiatives, Experts in the field – With the ability to shape decisions made at the grassroots level and Academicians – Whose experience in the field could provide valuable insights to the participants. It ensures a cooperative rather than competitive environment for the finalists during their stint at IIM Calcutta.

The event is to be conducted in 2 categories:

Participants who are working on an ongoing project for >6 months and <2 years
Participants who are working on an ongoing project for <6 months

Round 1: The participants need to send the details of their current project in a presentation format for evaluation.
Round 2: The shortlisted candidates need to present their project on campus to a panel comprising of corporates, experts and academicians.
