About

Strategy is about making choices, trade-offs; it’s about deliberately choosing to be different. Strategy is at the heart of every decision that organizations take to beat the competition. To win in the long run, strategy is no longer an option but the only answer

Consulting knights is here to judge your consulting skills! It challenges the participants with a comprehensive case developed with our corporate partners that tests the ability to deep dive into the problem and problem solve in a structured way to deliver effective and implementable solutions

Event Format

Round 1

An online round that tests the participants’ business understanding and acumen for case analysis

Round 2

A case based round where teams will display their mettle by solving an intriguing business problem

Round 3

The selected teams are then required to present their solution in front of our corporate partners on the final day

Event Rules

  • Registration is allowed in teams of sizes 3 or 4
  • Team members have to be from the same college
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • Individuals can not be a part of more than one team
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
