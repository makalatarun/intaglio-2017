About

The world of money is addictive and mesmerising. Be it personal, corporate or national, finance has always been at the top of our minds when it comes to decision making. FinGod explores two aspects of the financial system, Mergers & Acquisitions as well as the Derivatives world, both of which have been used to generate volumes of trade and wealth for individuals and corporations alike. Do you have what it takes to emerge victorious in this fast-paced, roller coaster ride?

Event Format

Round 1

An online quiz testing various concepts of finance

Round 2

Powerpoint submission of a M&A and/or Derivatives case

Round 3

Top teams will be selected to present their cases on campus during Intaglio
