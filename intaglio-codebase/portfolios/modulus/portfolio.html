About

The world of trading has evolved beyond recognition over the last few decades. What comprised of paperwork and outcry is now HFT and algorithms. Simply understanding the signals is not enough, but timing the trades to perfection as well is what makes a successful trader. Modulus aims at placing candidates in the shoes of a trader both in the past and in the present to test them on their market aptitude. May you have bull-run of a lifetime.

Event Format

Round 1

An online quiz that tests the aptitude on trading and investing, as well as basic concepts and knowledge of the markets

Round 2

An online simulation of trading on real market instruments over a course of 3 - 4 weeks.

Round 3

An interactive outcry trading round as well as a presentation of their trading strategies in Round 2

Event Rules

  • Teams will have to comprise of 3 members
  • There is no limit to the number of entries from a college
  • Individuals can not be a part of more than one team
  • The decisions taken by the organisers are final
